Our Mission

At FreeAI Education, our goal is to democratize artificial intelligence education. Founded by Rishi Gandhi, a Grade 11 student at Stone Hill International School in Bangalore, the company aims to provide high-quality, free resources and training in AI for students, teachers, and anyone interested in simplifying their tasks through technology.

Meet the Facilitator

Rishi Gandhi is passionate about AI and its potential to transform the world. He has completed various courses and seminars on artificial intelligence and holds a certification from the Vjal Institute. With a strong belief in the power of open education, Rishi founded FreeAI Education to make AI learning accessible to all.
